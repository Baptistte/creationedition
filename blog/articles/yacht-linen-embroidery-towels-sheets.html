Yacht Linen Embroidery: Custom Towels, Sheets & Cushions on the French Riviera

📅 9 March 2026⏱️ 6 min read✍️ Création Édition & Broderie

From monogrammed bath towels to logo-embroidered cushions, elevate your yacht's interior with bespoke linen embroidery. Based in Fréjus, serving the entire French Riviera.

Why Custom Linen Embroidery Matters on a Yacht

On a superyacht, every detail speaks to quality. Personalized linen creates a cohesive, luxurious atmosphere that distinguishes your vessel. Guests notice and remember the monogrammed towels, the logo on the cushions, the yacht name on the table runner.

What We Embroider

Bath Towels & Beach Towels

  • Yacht logo or name embroidered in matching thread colours
  • Guest monograms for VIP cabins
  • Size options: Hand towels, bath towels, beach towels, pool towels
  • Thread: Premium polyester — salt-water resistant, colour-fast

Bed Linen & Pillowcases

  • Monogram on pillowcases: Classic elegant touch
  • Yacht name on duvet covers: Subtle positioning near the hem
  • Thread colours: Tone-on-tone for understated luxury

Cushions & Outdoor Textiles

  • Deck cushion covers with embroidered yacht logo
  • Outdoor-grade thread: UV-resistant, waterproof
  • Table runners and napkins for formal dining

🎯 Turnaround Times

Standard: 5-7 working days. Express: 48-72 hours (subject to volumes). We understand the yachting industry runs on tight schedules. Drop-off and pick-up possible in Fréjus, Saint-Raphaël, Antibes and Cannes.

Quality & Durability

  • Industrial embroidery machines for consistent quality across all pieces
  • 500+ wash cycles without fading or fraying
  • Salt-water resistant polyester threads
  • Machine washable up to 60°C
